Company information

In implementation of the provisions of Article Two of the issuance of Law No. (203) of 1991 promulgating the Public Business Sector Companies Law, the Holding Company for Maritime Transport replaced the Public Sector Authority for Maritime Transport established by Presidential Decree No. (431) of 1983, and the Holding Company for Land Transport replaced the Public Sector Authority For Land and River Transport established by Presidential Decree No. (430) for the year 1983 and based on Prime Minister Decree No. 1499 for the year 2000, the Land Transport Holding Company has been merged into the Maritime Transport Holding Company. Amending the name of the company after the merger to become the “Holding Company for Maritime and Land Transport” an Egyptian joint stock holding company (SAE).

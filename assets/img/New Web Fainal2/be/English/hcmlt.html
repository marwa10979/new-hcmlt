The company provides the best and latest services for the convenience of its customers (WIFI - GPS - screens in the back of chairs - toilets - refrigerator - hospitality). The company has more than 48 locations that vary between (branches - workshops and garages - operating centers) at the level of the Republic in order to serve its huge fleet of cars. The company is one of the largest passenger transport companies in the Arab Republic of Egypt, where its services cover the governorates of Upper Egypt, the Red Sea, the New Valley, in addition to linking the governorates of East Delta, Central and West Delta, the cities of the Canal and Sinai with regular lines, through its 98 lines at the level of country
